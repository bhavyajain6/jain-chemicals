About Jain Chemicals

We are a Jaipur-based trading and distribution company specializing in the supply of industrial raw materials such as PVC resin, CPVC resin, plasticizers, calcium carbonate, stabilizers, pigments, titanium dioxide, phthalic anhydride, and other essential chemicals. Serving key sectors like PVC pipe manufacturing, CPVC pipe fittings, footwear, wire & cable, packaging, masterbatch, roto-moulding, rubber, and pharma industries, we ensure consistent quality, timely delivery, and competitive pricing.

Recognized as one of the best chemical suppliers in Jaipur, Rajasthan, we have built trusted partnerships with leading principals and bring decades of expertise in the chemicals and polymers industry. You can get all the chemicals for wires and cables, PVC, rubber, and footwear under one roof with us.

We provide not only raw materials but also technical guidance, application support, and cost-effective solutions tailored to customer requirements. Our customer-first approach has made us a reliable partner for manufacturers across plastics, footwear, wires, cables, packaging, and allied industries throughout India.
